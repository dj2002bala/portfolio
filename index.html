Our Story

A Passion for Perfect Coffee

Founded in 2019 by two friends who believed great coffee could bring people together, The Roasted Bean started as a tiny cart at the local farmers market.

Today, we've grown into a beloved neighborhood gathering spot, but our mission remains the same: serve exceptional coffee, support sustainable farming, and create a space where everyone feels at home.
